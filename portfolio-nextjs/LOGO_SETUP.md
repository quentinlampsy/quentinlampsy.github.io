# University Logos Setup Instructions

## Download University Logos

You need to add the following logo files to complete your education section:

### 1. Beijing Normal University Logo
- **File name**: `bnu-logo.png`
- **Location**: Save to `portfolio-nextjs/public/pictures/`
- **Where to get it**: 
  - Official: https://english.bnu.edu.cn/ (look for logo/brand assets)
  - Or search "Beijing Normal University logo png" and download a high-quality transparent PNG

### 2. The Chinese University of Hong Kong Logo
- **File name**: `cuhk-logo.png`  
- **Location**: Save to `portfolio-nextjs/public/pictures/`
- **Where to get it**:
  - Official: https://www.cuhk.edu.hk/ (look for logo/brand assets)
  - Or search "CUHK logo png" and download a high-quality transparent PNG

## Recommended Logo Specifications
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Size**: At least 512x512 pixels (will be displayed at 128x128px)
- **Aspect ratio**: Square or close to square works best

If you can't find the logos, you can also:

1. Place both logo files in `portfolio-nextjs/public/pictures/`

3. The logos will automatically appear in the Education section!
